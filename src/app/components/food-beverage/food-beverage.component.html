bistro dining and bar in Elvira

The best of tropical dining with a passion for great food, seasonality, creative cocktails, open kitchen design and a relaxed atmosphere – it’s what makes us unique

Fresh Favorite: Start the day with our take on the traditional Eggs Benedict with a Sri Lankan twist! Spicy Eggs Benny – poached eggs on spicy coconut roti, spinach, curried hollandaise, sausages, bacon and chili sambol.

Comfort Classic: Our catch of the day paired with seasonal produce to create the brightest and freshest salads, our menu brings you the dishes that speak to your heart.

Ocean Fresh: Tamarind glazed seared Yellow-fin tuna served with steamed rice, fresh Gotu Kola sambol and curry sauce

Sweet Delights: An array of daily dessert specials reflect what’s in season but our menu always features Sesame and cashew cream cheesecake – and of course decadent chocolate biscuit.
